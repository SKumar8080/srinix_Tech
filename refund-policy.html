Refund Policy

1. Digital Products

For all digital products and services offered by Srinix Technology:

  • Refund requests must be made within 3 days of purchase
  • Refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days

  • No refunds will be provided after the 3-day period

2. Physical Products

For all physical products offered by Srinix Technology:

  • Refund requests must be made within 7 days of delivery

  • Shipping costs for returns are the responsibility of the customer

3. Exceptions

The following items are not eligible for refunds:

  • Customized or personalized products
  • Products that have been used or damaged
  • Digital products that have been downloaded or accessed
  • Services that have already been rendered

4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund:
